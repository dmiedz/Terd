  1. Debellis (+1): Debellis has quietly put together an undefeated season through week 4, despite barely being in the top half of the league in points scored. It’s too early to tell if his team is good or just lucky, but to his credit, even winning a wrestling match against a girl still counts as a win.

  2. Davies (+2): Davies had another massive week, throttling lowly Danny. It was a waste of a week as he could have scored 40 less points and still won. At any rate, it won’t matter because Davies has a cake walk to the D1 bye as the only threatening team.

However, most impressive was Dac owning 3 bench players who all scored 20 points.

Incredibly, both are still ranked first and second in the league. On the plus side, Isaac plays 0-4 Ben this week, but on the negative side, Isaac’s Rona RB is now starting to take down the rest of his team as collateral damage.
